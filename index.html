MarketMinds Global Launches "Insightify" Copilot to Eliminate 72-Hour Bottleneck

Summary

MarketMinds Global today announced the internal rollout and 60-day customer pilot of Insightify Copilot, an advanced Generative AI-powered workspace engineered specifically for market research analysts. By integrating real-time ingestion pipelines with a hyper-secure Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) architecture, Insightify automates the manual sifting of millions of unstructured data points—including global news, social media chatter, analyst reports, and forum discussions. The platform compresses the current 72-hour comprehensive report lifecycle to under 24 hours, completely eliminating analyst burnout and elevating client insight actionability back to elite tier performance standards.

"Our current market intelligence engine is bottlenecked by manual data processing, causing our Time-to-Insight metric to balloon from 24 hours to an unacceptable 72 hours over the last six months. This operational lag has caused our client Actionability Score to plummet from 4.5/5 to 3.0/5, triggering an alarming spike in client churn to nimbler competitors. Concurrently, our elite research analysts are processing thousands of documents monthly completely by hand, resulting in severe professional burnout and a dramatic decline in operational accuracy." — Elena Rodriguez, VP of Product Strategy
