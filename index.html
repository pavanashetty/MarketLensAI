Deliverable 1.2: Problem Discovery

The Competitive Gap & Churn Catalyst

The Problem Vector

  • •
    Manual Sifting Latency: It takes 72 hours of manual analyst sifting through news, financial filings, and trade sheets to compile comprehensive intelligence reports.
  • •
    Strategic Information Blind Spots: Opportunities and competitive threats are detected too late, providing retrospective rather than predictive foresight.
  • •
    Analyst Burnout Loop: Overwhelming information fatigue forces analysts to spend 40-50% of their billable hours on simple mechanical data extraction.

The MarketLens Resolution

  • •
    Automated Multi-Source Scraper: Instantly aggregates and filters structured and unstructured data across news networks, filings, publications, and social conversations.
  • •
    RAG Real-Time Synthesis: Reduces Time-to-Insight (TTI) down from 72 hours to under 24 hours (and under 15 minutes for targeted queries) using semantic embeddings.
  • •
    Explainable Citations: Enforces source validation through a dedicated citation attribution layer, completely bypassing the risks of factual hallucination.

Three Critical Framework Questions

1. WHAT NEEDS TO CHANGE?

Manual mechanical synthesis, retrospective reporting, late identification of trends, and the massive billable hours leak on repetitive document sifting.

2. WHAT WILL REMAIN?

Trusted expert domain knowledge, analyst-led rigorous verification protocols, human oversight, and the high prestige client relationship paradigm.

3. WHAT'S NEWLY POSSIBLE?

Real-time predictive trend detection, automated white-space discovery trackers, and instant cited threat briefs compiled on demand by GenAI pipelines.
